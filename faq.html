Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we receive about our cacti and services. If you don’t find an answer to your query, feel free to reach out to us.

How do I care for my cactus?

Cacti are easy to care for, but they do need some specific attention. They prefer bright sunlight and well-drained soil. Water them once every two weeks and ensure that they aren’t overwatered.

Can I plant my cactus outside?

Yes, many types of cacti can thrive outdoors in warm climates. Ensure that the location receives plenty of sunlight and that the soil drains well to prevent root rot.

What is the best soil for cacti?

Cacti need well-draining soil. You can use a special cactus mix or mix regular potting soil with sand and perlite to improve drainage.

Do you offer a warranty on your plants?

Yes, we offer a satisfaction guarantee. If your plant arrives damaged or unhealthy, please contact us within 7 days for a replacement or refund.

How long will it take for my order to arrive?

Orders are typically processed within 2-3 business days, and shipping can take 5-7 days depending on your location.

Can I change my order after it’s been placed?

Unfortunately, once an order is placed, we cannot make changes. However, if there’s an issue with your order, please contact our customer service team, and we will assist you as best as we can.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship internationally. International shipping costs will vary depending on your location and the size of the order.

How do I know which cactus is best for my home?

Consider the lighting and temperature in your home. Some cacti, like the saguaro, need more space and sunlight, while others, like the barrel cactus, are perfect for smaller spaces with indirect light.
