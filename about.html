About Us

Welcome to CaCti, your number one source for ornamental plants. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of cacti and succulents, with an emphasis on quality, uniqueness, and sustainability.

We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly wing of the plant industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
